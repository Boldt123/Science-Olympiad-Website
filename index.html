2017-18 SO Season

Although the Jenison Science Olympiad team did not move onto State competition this year, our team did see some success in individual events. Winning medals in their events were Maddie McCluggage, Gretchen DeBoer, Joey Shotts, Quinten Burlew, Ethan Brower, Jaden Henderson, Lizzy Lee, Gabe Ter Haar, Luca Pham, Casey DuBois and Hannah Wellman. Special recognition to Lizzy Lee and Ethan Brower for a 1st place medal in Road Scholar, and Maddie McCluggage, Ethan Brower and Jaden Henderson for medalling in multiple events.
