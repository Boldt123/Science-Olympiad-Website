Student Resources

A quick note for incoming seventh graders: For specific resources, a simple google search is often irreplacable. Scioly.org is the best resource for event-specific questions or learning about an event in general. Visit the forums here and the wiki here. We use Discord, an online communication platform similar to Skype or Teamspeak, to communicate. Ask any Eighth Grade or High School student for an invite. Other than this, you can email questions directly to your coaches or ask more experienced students (e.g. high schoolers) on the Science Olympiad discord.

Here's an archive of things students have requested to be kept on the resources page. Among other things, we've kept Mini-Expert presentations, which are presentations given by students about certain topics in an event. If you would like to see something on the resources page, ask Jaden Henderson on Discord, in person or over email (2022jhenders@jpsstudents.org).
